About CSBS Department

The Department of Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) at Maharaja Institute of Technology Mysore was established with the objective of creating industry-ready professionals who possess strong foundations in computer science along with business and management skills.

The CSBS program is designed in collaboration with industry to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. It integrates Computer Science, Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Business Management, and Entrepreneurship, enabling students to develop both technical expertise and business acumen.
